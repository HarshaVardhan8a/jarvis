Diligent Corporation is a leading governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) SaaS company.
Key Facts:
- Headquarters: New York, NY.
- Mission: To empower leaders with technology, insights, and analytics to drive greater impact and accountability.
- Products: Diligent Boards, Diligent Entities, Diligent ESG, Diligent Audit, and more.
- Diligent One Platform: A unified platform that brings together governance, risk, compliance, and audit data.
- User Base: Over 700,000 board members and leaders rely on Diligent software.
- Focus: Modern Governance, providing secure communication and collaboration tools for executives and board members.
